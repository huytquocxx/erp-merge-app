## Deploy Your App (2 minutes):

1. **Go to Streamlit Cloud:**
   https://share.streamlit.io/

2. **Sign in with GitHub:**
   - Click "Sign in"
   - Authorize Streamlit to access your GitHub

3. **Create New App:**
   - Click "New app" button
   - Fill in:
     - **Repository:** `huytquocxx/erp-merge-app`
     - **Branch:** `main`
     - **Main file path:** `erp_merge_app.py`
   - Click **"Deploy"**

4. **Wait 1-2 minutes** for deployment...

5. **Your app will be live at:**
   `https://erp-merge-app.streamlit.app` (or similar URL)
